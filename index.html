HANGING WITH MY FLUFFY FRIENDS

  • How to get
    Make a camp and feed the dogs until their hunger level is maxed.
  • How to get
    Make a camp and pet the dogs.

The FOX

  • How to get
    Event: Can you actually find food like that?
    Choose "I want to see if you can pull this off.", then keep on watching the fox. At the end, there will be a chance that the fox will catch a prey. Choose "I can conquer this too. Thanks for the inspiration." and "Thanks, foxy." to get the journal entry.
  • How to get
    Event: Can you actually find food like that?
    Choose "I want to see if you can pull this off.", then keep on watching the fox. At the end, there will be a chance that the fox will give up.

The BEAR

  • How to get
    Event: Ok, so there's just a creepy abandoned cabin sitting over there. Um...
    Choose anything for the first option, then choose "I can handle whatever's in there!" for the second option. There will be a chance that a bear will be in the cabin.
  • How to get
    Event: Aww, a little bear cub!
    Choose "Let's follow it and see.", then "Let's watch and see what happens.". There will be a chance that a mama bear will show up and give the cub a kiss.
  • How to get
    Event: Aww, a little bear cub!
    Choose "Let's follow it and see.", then "Let's watch and see what happens.". There will be a chance that a mama bear will show up from the right side of the screen and attack you.
  • How to get
    Event: Aww, a little bear cub!
    Choose "Let's follow it and see.", then "Let's watch and see what happens.". There will be a chance that the cub will use a nearby tree to scratch its back.

The WEASEL

  • How to get
    Event: Huh? Would you look at that...
    This is Bodega's first story event. Choose "Follow them." for the journal entry.

The OWL

  • How to get
    Event: I'm certain I read that owls are good omens.
    Choose "Yeah, owl good things come to those who wait.", "Let's follow it.", and "Watch the owl.". There is a chance that the owl will throw up a pellet that contains a bullet.
  • How to get
    Event: I'm certain I read that owls are good omens.
    Choose "Didn't they mean certain death?", "Let's follow it.", and "I'm going to shoot you now.". Kill the owl.
  • How to get
    Event: Chomper, don't get any bad ideas.
    Choose "Follow them", and then "Watch and see what happens.". There will be a chance that the owl will catch the skunk.

The RABBIT

  • How to get
    Event: Rabbit is supposed to be a delicacy.
    Choose "Check it out.", and then either let the timer runs out or choose "You mind being bait?".

The MOOSE

  • How to get
    Event: You can't see anything out there.
    Choose "Check out.", and then "Leave.".
  • How to get
    Event: There's an owl nearby somewhere.
    Choose "Let's go look for the owl".
  • How to get
    Event: You pups see that? It's a caribou!
    Choose "Let's watch it.", "Try a caribou call", and "Moo.". There will be a chance for a moose to show up.

The EAGLE

  • How to get
    Event: Did you see that?! A bald eagle!
    Choose "Chase it.", and either wait for the timer to run out or "Wait.".
  • How to get
    Event: Did you see that?! A bald eagle!
    Choose either "Hunt it." or "Chase it.", and then "Shoot it anyway.".

The DEER

  • How to get
    Event: Is there something up there?
    Choose "Inspect it.", "Approach it.", and then leave the scene (do not harvest the meat).
  • How to get
    Event: Something is watching us...
    Choose "Check it out.", and "Raise rifle.". There will be a chance that the deer will survive your shot, regardless if you missed or not in the shooting minigame.

The CARIBOU

  • How to get
    Event: You pups see that? It's a caribou!
    Choose "Let's watch it.", "Try a caribou call", and "Grunt.". There will be a chance that the caribou will be interested in your call and approach you.
  • How to get
    Event: Wait, is that a caribou?!...
    Choose "Let's hunt.". Shoot the caribou, and when you are prompted to choose an option, let the timer runs out.
  • How to get
    Event: Wait, is that a caribou?!...
    Choose "Let's hunt.". Shoot the caribou, and either leave or miss the shot (just click outside the circle in the shooting minigame).
  • How to get
    When you meet a caribou in one of the events, try to shoot it, but click outside the circle in the shooting minigame to miss the shot. So far, these events are confirmed to work:
    • Event: Wait, is that a caribou?!...
      Choose "Let's hunt.". Shoot the caribou, and either leave or miss the shot (just click outside the circle in the shooting minigame).
    • Event: You pups see that? It's a caribou!
      Choose "Let's hunt it.". Miss the shot (just click outside the circle in the shooting minigame).

    Once you finish either event, there will be a chance for this event to spawn.
    Event: What's that out there?
  • How to get
    Event: You pups see that?
    Choose "Follow it.".

The SQUIRREL

  • How to get
    Event: Oh great, a squirrel.
    This is Chomper's first story event. Choose "Fine, let's check it out.", and then "I speak wink. I'm gonna wink back.". There will be a chance that the squirrel will attack you.
  • How to get
    Event: Ok, so there's just a creepy abandoned cabin sitting over there. Um...
    Choose any option for the first two options (just make sure to enter the cabin). There will be a chance for a squirrel to be inside the cabin. Choose "Let's talk this out.".
  • How to get
    You need to finish this event first:
    Event: Oh great, a squirrel.
    This is Chomper's first story event. Choose "Fine, let's check it out.", and then "I'm gonna shot it. (Use bullet)". Kill the squirrel, and choose "Just leave it. Maybe it will throw off a predator.".

    Once you finish that event, there will be a chance for this event to spawn:
    Event: Chomper...
    Choose "Fine. We'll go check it out.", and "Ok, fine, it's your nemesis. Go try to eat it.". There will be a chance that a bunch of squirrels will show up. Choose "Dogs, snarl at them or something.".

The WOLVERINE

  • How to get
    Event: Feel free to stay over there. We don't mind.
    This is either Igloo's second story event or Bodega's third story event.

    Choose "Let's check it out.", and then harvest either 1 or 2 meats. There will be a chance that a wolverine will spot you.

The WOLF

    This is Noodle's first story event. Choose "Try to find it.", and then "Oh, you just wanted to help.".
  • How to get
    Event: Oh hey... a little wolf friend.
    The journal can only be triggered when Barkley still hasn't finished their story events. Choose "Throw food at wolf.".
  • How to get

    Choose "Follow it.", and then "Go get it.". There will be a chance that a wolf will show up and attack you.
